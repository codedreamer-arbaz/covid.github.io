What is COVID-19 (Corona-Virus)

COVID-19 is the infectious disease caused by the most recently discovered coronavirus. This new virus and dieases were unknown before the outbreak began in Wuhan, Chaina, in December 2019.

Coronaviruses are a large family of viruses which may couse illness in animals or humans. In humans, several coronaviruses are known to cause repiratory infections ranging from the common cold to more severe diseses such as Middle East Respiratory Syndrom (MERS) and Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome (SARS). The most recently discovered coronavirus causes coronavirus dieses COVID-19.

6 Steps Prevention Against Coronavirus

Wash Your hands regularly for 20 seconds, with soap and water or sanitizer's.

Cover your nose and mouth with a disposable tissue or flexed elebow when you cough or sneeze.

Avoid close contact (1 meter or 3 feet) with people who are unwell.

Stay home and self-isolate from other in the household if you feel unwell.

Stay informed by watching news & follow the recommended practices.

If you have fever,cough and difficulty in breathing seek for medical care early.
